Creamy Pasta


Description

There's nothing like a creamy pasta dish for cold winter nights or really any time of the year; pasta, in its endless variability, transcends the seasons. This creamy pasta recipe is great for all skill levels.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cups chicken broth, or more as needed
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ pound spaghetti
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¾ cup heavy cream
  • 1 ½ tablespoons dried parsley
